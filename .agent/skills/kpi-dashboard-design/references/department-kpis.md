# Common KPIs by Department

## Sales KPIs

### Revenue Metrics
*   **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)**: Predictable revenue.
*   **Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)**: MRR * 12.
*   **Average Revenue Per User (ARPU)**: Total Revenue / Total Users.
*   **Revenue Growth Rate**: Month-over-month growth.

### Pipeline Metrics
*   **Sales Pipeline Value**: Total value of open opportunities.
*   **Win Rate**: Closed Won / Total Closed.
*   **Average Deal Size**: Revenue / Number of Deals.
*   **Sales Cycle Length**: Average days from Lead to Close.

## Marketing KPIs

### Acquisition
*   **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)**: Marketing Spend / New Customers.
*   **CPA (Cost Per Acquisition)**: Cost per action (lead, sale).
*   **MQL (Marketing Qualified Leads)**: Leads ready for sales.

### Engagement & ROI
*   **Conversion Rate**: Visitors who take action.
*   **Marketing ROI**: (Revenue - Cost) / Cost.
*   **CAC Payback Period**: Months to recover CAC.

## Product KPIs

### Usage
*   **DAU/MAU**: Daily/Monthly Active Users (Stickiness).
*   **Session Duration**: Average time spent in app.
*   **Feature Adoption**: % of users using a specific feature.

### Quality & Growth
*   **NPS (Net Promoter Score)**: User loyalty.
*   **Retention Rate**: % of users returning after X days.
*   **Churn Rate**: % of users lost in a period.

## Finance KPIs

### Profitability
*   **Gross Margin**: (Revenue - COGS) / Revenue.
*   **EBITDA**: Earnings Before Interest, Taxes, Depreciation, Amortization.
*   **Net Profit Margin**: Net Income / Revenue.

### Efficiency
*   **Burn Rate**: Cash spent per month.
*   **Runway**: Cash Balance / Burn Rate.
*   **LTV/CAC Ratio**: Lifetime Value / Acquisition Cost (Target > 3).
